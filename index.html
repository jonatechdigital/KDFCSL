About Us

The Cooperative That's Changing Rural Uganda

For over 50 years, Kimwanyi Dairy Farmers' Cooperative has been a beacon of hope and empowerment for vulnerable communities in Nakaseke, Uganda. Located just a two-hour drive from Kampala, we are dedicated to uplifting women, youth, and the elderly through sustainable agricultural practices and community support.

Supporting Vulnerable Members of Our Community

At Kimwanyi Dairy Farmers' Cooperative, we are dedicated to uplifting vulnerable members of our community, including women, youth, and the elderly. Our programs provide essential resources and training to help them thrive. Our programs are designed to provide direct solutions to the specific challenges they face.

Empowering Women Farmers

Supporting Our Elders

Key Sectors

Transforming Lives Through Agriculture

Our four flagship programs - Coffee, Maize, Dairy and SAACO - create complete value chains that empower farmers from seed to market. Through comprehensive training, quality inputs, and guaranteed market access, we're building prosperity in rural communities.

COFFEE:

From seedling to cup - we guide coffee farmers through cultivation, processing, quality control, and direct market connections for premium prices.

Provide expert training on sustainable Coffee farming

Forge direct market links to guarantee fair, consistent, and competitive pay

Provide access to quality farm inputs, including high-yield seedlings and organic fertilizers.

MAIZE:

Boosting yields through improved seeds, modern techniques, and collective marketing that secures better prices for our maize farmers.

Add value to raw grain with our modern maize processing plant for higher profits

Secure better prices and more favorable terms through collective marketing

Facilitate access to quality seeds and climate-smart training to increase harvests.

DAIRY:

Quality breeds, best practices training, and milk collection systems that ensure consistent income for our dairy farmers.

Introduce high-yield Friesian cows to multiply daily milk production and income.

Teach advanced dairy management to improve herd health and overall farm output.

Run an organized collection system that provides a stable and dependable market.

SACCO:

The financial engine of our cooperative, providing access to capital that unlocks the potential of our members, especially women.

Equip members with financial literacy skills to build profitable farm enterprises

Provide accessible loans and credit to overcome the primary barrier to farm growth.

Fund on-farm investments like improved seeds, quality livestock, and equipment.

Testimonial

Stories of Change

Here's how our partnership with farmers creates tangible, life-changing impact, told in their own words.

"The cooperative has empowered us to take control of our livelihoods. As a woman, getting a loan was impossible. But through the KDFCS women's SACCO, I secured a loan to expand my coffee garden with the high-yield seedlings they provided. After my first big harvest, the cooperative's direct market connection ensured I got a premium price for my beans."
Amina Nansubuga
Coffee Farmer

"Selling raw maize to middlemen meant low and unpredictable prices. Joining the cooperative gave me access to their milling machine and training on post-harvest handling. Now, I produce high-quality maize flour, and KDFCS provides a stable market with a premium price. This has brought real security to my family and allows me to plan for the future."

Joseph Kato
Maize Producer
